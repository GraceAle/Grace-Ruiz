Here are my 5 milestones of Apple

The iPhone

The iPhone was the first phone create by apple. The iPhone was released June 24, 2007. It started at $499 for just 4GB. Back then 4GB seemed a lot because not many apps were existed and not much but just texting and taking photos was a thing back then. It was a very small device and to be fair it was fairly decent in 2007. It offered a 3.7-in screen with a 2 mega-pixel camera. Many people were worried the iPhone wouldn’t sell because of its price. But with little time sells grew rapidly. The iPhone laid the foundation for the modern smartphone. It allowed great access to the world’s information. Many were fascinated on how the device was touch screen. The Apple store also made a big difference. Many people were granted access to many amazing apps at the time that helped people of that time. Of course, today we would have thought that was so little.

iPhone 4

4 years after The iPhone the iPhone 4 was announced. It was quickly released June 24, 2010. It was completely redesigned and came with a lot of news features. The arrival of facetime was brought with the iPhone 4. Facetime is something huge many people used and even is used a lot today. The iPhone 4 was the first phone to have a retina put in place. It provided up to 32 GB. The price for the phone ranged from $200-300.

iPhone 7

Apple came out with a new design for the iPhone 7 in 2016. The iphone 8 was the first iphone to have a glass back when normally they were made out of aluminum. the biggest problem with a glass back people had was that it was easily breakable. It also made the phone heavier and easier to drop. Replacing the glass on the back could be as expensive as buying a new phone. The camera quality was improved by adding a 12-mega pixel. A big change in this iphone was the home button. The home button was changed into a haptic-based virtual button. The audio jack was also removed, so any old headphones wouldn’t work unless an adapter were to be connected to the headphones. Quickly after announcing the Iphone 8 it was released Sept. 22 starting at $699.

iPhone X

Not a year later after the iphone 8 the iPhone X was announced sept. 12, 2017. This was one of apples most expensive iPhones to come out. It was completely redesigned with a new glass that was stainless. Face id was put into place and did a favor of removing the home button. Face id is just as safe as the home button. Except to enter your phone while locked you’d have to get your whole face into your front camera. Face id also includes an auto image which betters the front-facing camera. Its GB’s also increased and its highest was 256 but it came with a greater cost. Animoji was also a new thing that came with the new ios. An Animoji was just a custom version of popular emojis and the face id allowed the system to be abke to create a 3D emoji of your face. the iPhone X was finally released a couple days after it was announced on November 3rd starting at $999.

iPhone 12

Recently apple announced the new iPhone 12 on October 13, 2020. The iPhone 12 provided a 6.7-inch screen. The iPhone 12 pro is the biggest iphone we have so far. It goes up to 512 GB. Its screen provide a new super retina XDR, which was built like a strong shield for better luck for it not breaking. Its 3 lens pro 12MP camera system provides better video quality, different lighting conditions, and zoom effects on the camera app. The iPhone 12 was released Oct. 23, 2020 at $1099. The ios 14 recently came out with it as well. It provided new widgets on the home screen and it helped better organize apps. Before entering an app it will ask you if you want the app to track you and with a simple click you can decide. Siri was redesigned to answer calls and send messages for you.
